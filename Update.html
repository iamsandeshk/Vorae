One UI 7 Update Schedule: When Will Your Device Get It?

February 23, 2025
Update Schedule One UI 7 Galaxy Devices

Samsung has outlined its comprehensive update schedule for One UI 7, detailing which devices will receive the update and when. The rollout will be phased to ensure stability and optimize the experience for each device.

Phase 1 (March 2025):

• Galaxy S24 Series (S24, S24+, S24 Ultra)
• Galaxy Z Fold5
• Galaxy Z Flip5

Phase 2 (April 2025):

• Galaxy S23 Series (S23, S23+, S23 Ultra)
• Galaxy Z Fold4
• Galaxy Z Flip4
• Galaxy Tab S9 Series

Phase 3 (May-June 2025):

• Galaxy S22 Series
• Galaxy Z Fold3
• Galaxy Z Flip3
• Galaxy A54, A53
• Galaxy Tab S8 Series

The exact timing may vary by region and carrier. Users can check their device's eligibility and expected update timeline through the Samsung Members app.
