Visual Overhaul: One UI 7's Fresh New Look

February 23, 2025
Design One UI 7 UI/UX

One UI 7 introduces a significant visual refresh, building upon Samsung's design philosophy while introducing modern elements and improved usability. The update brings subtle yet impactful changes to the interface, making it more intuitive and visually appealing.

The most notable design changes include:

• Refined Typography: New dynamic font scaling and improved readability
• Enhanced Motion Design: Smoother transitions and more natural animations
• Updated Color Palette: New accent colors and improved dark mode
• Redesigned Icons: More consistent and modern icon design language
• Improved Layout: Better use of space and enhanced one-handed usage

The Quick Settings panel has been completely redesigned, featuring larger, more accessible toggles and improved organization options. The notification shade now includes expanded media controls and smart device shortcuts, making it easier to control your connected ecosystem.

Samsung has also introduced new customization options, allowing users to personalize their experience further. This includes new widget styles, enhanced theme support, and more granular control over system animations and transitions.

One UI 7 Update Schedule: When Will Your Device Get It?

February 23, 2025
Update Schedule One UI 7 Galaxy Devices

Samsung has outlined its comprehensive update schedule for One UI 7, detailing which devices will receive the update and when. The rollout will be phased to ensure stability and optimize the experience for each device.

Phase 1 (March 2025):

• Galaxy S24 Series (S24, S24+, S24 Ultra)
• Galaxy Z Fold5
• Galaxy Z Flip5

Phase 2 (April 2025):

• Galaxy S23 Series (S23, S23+, S23 Ultra)
• Galaxy Z Fold4
• Galaxy Z Flip4
• Galaxy Tab S9 Series

Phase 3 (May-June 2025):

• Galaxy S22 Series
• Galaxy Z Fold3
• Galaxy Z Flip3
• Galaxy A54, A53
• Galaxy Tab S8 Series

The exact timing may vary by region and carrier. Users can check their device's eligibility and expected update timeline through the Samsung Members app.
